Thank you

From the Toymaker Krewe, thank you so much for participating in this event. It fills our hearts with joy to see the community come together at this joyous time to celebrate Wintersday.

What can I gift?

Anything as long as the total value of your gift exceeds 10g and will fit in one mail (5 items). Meaning you can for instance give your match a dagger which is worth 10g, or a set of runes that in total are worth 10g. And naturally, there is no max limit, so if you feel extremely generous and you are one wealthy skritt, you can go nuts!

Can I give more than one gift?

Yes! For those feeling extra generous, there's the option to donate additional gifts.

By clicking the button "Donate a gift", a random participant will be picked for you to gift. Remember, the same rules of gifting apply for donations, and you must send and mark all your current gifts as sent before being able to donate.
